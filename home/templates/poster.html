BACKGROUND AND SCOPE

Last year, the maiden Ghana Data Science Summit (IndabaX Ghana 2019) brought together about 200 people to learn, connect and network. The poster presentation session was very exciting! We saw posters on natural language processing, credit scoring (machine learning) , computer vision, applications of machine learning in agriculture to mention a few. Participants engaged the presenters through questioning, listening, giving feedback, exchanging contacts for possible collaborations, and what have you. The top three poster presenters were selected and awarded by Deep Learning Indaba, Google, and Microsoft respectively.
This year, we hope to receive more poster presentations as our participants for last year indicated they enjoyed and learned a lot. The poster abstracts submitted will undergo a thorough selection process to ensure the content is great and will be beneficial to our participants. We welcome submissions related but not limited to data science, machine learning, data mining, artificial intelligence. Posters that meet one or more of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are highly encouraged. Female participants are also highly encouraged to submit posters.



Possible Poster Topics Areas

  • Data Science
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Machine Learning
  • Big Data
  • Data Mining
  • Internet of Things (IOT)
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Business Intelligence
  • Computer Vision
  • Applications of machine learning

Steps to Submit your poster abstract

  1. Apply for the conference
  2. In the application, select to present a paper/poster and write a brief abstract about you paper.
  3. Done! Wait to receive notification on your selection!
